How was the deploying process?
In a brief explanation, after you sign in in Netlify, you just need to upload a .zip file containing a file named index.html. Then you go to a domain sercive like Namecheap and create/buy the domain with the designation that you desire. After the creation of the domain in Namecheap, you go to settings and copy the information about a thing called nameservers. You go back to Netlify and under the domain settings, you change the domain name for the one you created on Namecheap. After this, you can see that there is a section called nameservers. This is where you paste the nameservers copied from Namecheap. Et voilá, congratulations, you have now deployed your website.
